About Non-Semantic Markup
Non-semantic HTML is the use of HTML markup for presentation or look rather than to reinforce the semantics, or meaning, of the information in webpages and web applications.
Non-semantics is the non-study of meaning.
Drawbacks of Non-Semantic Markup
1. Hinders SEO
2. Hampers accessibility
3. Makes the code harder to read and maintain
